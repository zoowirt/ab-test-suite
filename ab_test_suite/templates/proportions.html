A/B-Test Suite: Comparing Proportions


Comparing two success rates

Scenario: in a series of trials, each experiment results in either success or failure. Examples are
  • e-commerce: showing products to a customer, the customer clicks (success) or does not click (failure)
  • sociology: a person is a non-smoker (success) or smoker (failure)
We assume that we have two groups A and B, with intrinsic success rates. For a number of trials and the measured number of successes in each group, we want to decide if we can deduce with a given confidence that the intrinsic rates differ.
